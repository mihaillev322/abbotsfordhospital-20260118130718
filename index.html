Excellence in Healthcare

Providing compassionate, comprehensive medical care to the Abbotsford community and surrounding areas for over three decades.

Our Medical Services

Emergency Department

Our 24/7 emergency department is staffed with experienced physicians, nurses, and support staff ready to handle medical emergencies of all types. We provide rapid assessment, stabilization, and treatment for critical conditions including heart attacks, strokes, trauma injuries, and severe infections. Our emergency team works closely with specialists throughout the hospital to ensure patients receive comprehensive care when every minute counts.

Surgical Services

Our state-of-the-art surgical suites accommodate a wide range of procedures from routine outpatient surgeries to complex operations. We offer orthopedic surgery, general surgery, cardiovascular procedures, and minimally invasive laparoscopic techniques. Our surgical team includes board-certified surgeons, anesthesiologists, and specialized nursing staff committed to patient safety and optimal outcomes.

Maternity Care

Our maternity ward provides comprehensive prenatal, delivery, and postnatal care in a comfortable, family-centered environment. We support natural births, cesarean deliveries, and high-risk pregnancies with specialized maternal-fetal medicine expertise. Our birthing suites are designed to accommodate families while maintaining the highest medical standards for mother and baby safety.

Diagnostic Imaging

Advanced diagnostic capabilities include MRI, CT scanning, ultrasound, X-ray, and nuclear medicine services. Our imaging department utilizes the latest technology to provide accurate, timely diagnoses that guide treatment decisions. Radiologists work collaboratively with referring physicians to ensure comprehensive interpretation and rapid communication of results.

Specialized Departments

Abbotsford Hospital houses numerous specialized departments staffed by expert medical professionals. Our cardiology department provides comprehensive heart care including diagnostic testing, interventional procedures, and cardiac rehabilitation programs. The oncology unit offers cancer treatment services with medical oncology, radiation therapy, and supportive care programs designed to help patients and families navigate their cancer journey.

Our orthopedic department specializes in bone, joint, and muscle disorders, providing both surgical and non-surgical treatments for conditions ranging from sports injuries to joint replacements. The neurology department diagnoses and treats disorders of the nervous system, including stroke care, epilepsy management, and movement disorders.

The intensive care unit provides critical care for patients requiring continuous monitoring and life support. Our ICU team includes intensivists, critical care nurses, respiratory therapists, and other specialists working around the clock to provide the highest level of care for critically ill patients.

Mental health services are integrated throughout our hospital system, with psychiatric consultation available for inpatients and emergency department patients. We recognize the importance of addressing both physical and mental health needs as part of comprehensive patient care.

Community Commitment

As a cornerstone of healthcare in the Fraser Valley, Abbotsford Hospital is deeply committed to serving our diverse community. We provide health education programs, community outreach initiatives, and preventive care services designed to improve overall population health. Our hospital partners with local schools, community organizations, and public health agencies to address health disparities and promote wellness throughout the region.

We understand that healthcare extends beyond our hospital walls, which is why we maintain strong relationships with family physicians, specialists, and other healthcare providers throughout the area. This collaborative approach ensures continuity of care and helps patients navigate the healthcare system effectively.
